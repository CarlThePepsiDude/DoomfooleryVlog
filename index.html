WELCOME

Deathmatch: Doomfooolery is a mod for DOOM 2 that combines humor, stupidity and memes with the average DOOM 2 Deathmatch gameplay. The mod doesn't only focuses on the memes, it comes with its own pack of maps!, and it is hoped to support every versus mode:

  • Deathmatch
  • Team Deathmatch
  • Capture The Flag

And not only that, but extra cool stuff that is yet to be revealed!!. More updates are yet to come... hope you like what has been done so far! If you want to keep up with the mod, you can check the news to see what it has been up to!
