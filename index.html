WELCOME

Deathmatch: Doomfoolery is a mod for DOOM 2 that combines humor and memes with Vanilla DOOM 2 Deathmatch gameplay.

This mod is packed with:

  • Simple and Goofy Art-Style.
  • Random sound effects from both old and modern memes!!, everyone is welcome.
  • Funny features like Taunt, Cursing and Sprays!.

Doomfoolery also has its own maps!, and multiple gamemode support!!, those being

  • Deathmatch (Done!)
  • Team Deathmatch (Done!)
  • Duel (Done!)

And hopefully more gamemodes will be supported, next target is Capture The Flag!

This mod's purpose is to bring some laughts and stupidity into your casual Deathmatch gameplays, by giving you stuff to toy with and unique details that will make you feel like you're inside of a doom machinima.
